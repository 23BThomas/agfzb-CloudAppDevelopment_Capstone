Welcome to Best Cars dealership, home to the best cars in North America. We sell domestic and imported cars at reasonable prices.


Best Cars dealership was founded in 2003 and is a family business employing four generations of the same family and over 30 staff. We at Best Cars dealership have built our reputation on customer service with over 50% of the business being loyal customers who keep coming back to us.

We are a 'Highly rated' Auto Trader dealer and a 'Top rated' Car Gurus dealer. We have over 180 cars on our site that cover a wide range of different types, models and styles, together with a purpose-built workshop, valet bay & 'smart' repair centre. The staff bring with them a wealth of experience from across the motor industry adding up to over 130 years of motor trade knowledge and experience.
